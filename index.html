About United Empire

United Empire is a New organisation in eSports, with professional teams in the most popular games such as:Battlefield 4,Call of Duty And More. Our players are representing our brand in every corner of the world.

Around the world United Empire stands as a lightweight in the world of eSports. Starting from now our players are looking for incredible success and to win numerous championships across multiple games.

The United Empire organisation was founded by both Alex Meleady and Trystan Greve.While staying true to our roots United Empire will continue to breed the best talent, select the finest partners, and continue to exceed expectations.
